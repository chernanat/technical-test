How reviews work
Reviews from past guests help our community learn more about each home. By default, reviews are sorted by relevancy. Relevancy is based on recency, length, and information that you provide to us, such as your booking search, your country, and your language preferences. Only the guest who booked the reservation can leave a review, and Airbnb only moderates reviews flagged for not following our policies. To be eligible for a percentile ranking or guest favorite label, listings need at least 5 reviews in the last 4 years. Criteria is subject to change.
